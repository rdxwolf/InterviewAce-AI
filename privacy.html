Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Information We Collect

Currently, InterviewAce AI operates as a static frontend application. We do not require you to create an account, and we do not actively collect personal identifiable information (PII) such as your name, address, or phone number unless you explicitly provide it to us via our Contact Form.

2. How We Use Your Data

Any data processed during your practice sessions (such as scores, selected categories, and progress) is handled locally within your web browser. We do not store your quiz results on external servers.

3. Contact Form Submissions

If you choose to use our Contact Us page, the information you provide (Name, Email, Message) is used solely to respond to your inquiry. We do not sell or share this information with third parties.

4. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites or community platforms. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those external sites.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time as we add new features (such as user accounts). We will notify users of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
