Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers. Here is everything you need to know about InterviewAce AI.

Is InterviewAce AI completely free to use?

Yes! We believe in accessible education for everyone. All of our practice categories, mock questions, and progress tracking features are 100% free to use.

Where do the interview questions come from?

Our questions are curated by industry professionals and educators. We constantly update our database based on real-world interview trends in tech, HR, and customer support.

Do I need to create an account to practice?

Currently, you can jump right in and start practicing without an account. Your progress is tracked locally during your session.

Can I suggest new questions or categories?

Absolutely! We love community feedback. You can reach out to us via the Contact page to suggest new topics or specific questions you'd like to see added.

How does the scoring system work?

At the end of your practice session, the system calculates your score based on the number of correct answers divided by the total number of questions attempted in that session.
